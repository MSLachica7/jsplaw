United States Visa and Immigration Assistance

K-1 or Fiancee Visa


The K-1 or fiancée visa petition, spouse visa petition and alien relative petition are a two-phase process. The first phase is the submission of the appropriate Petition to the USCIS. After approval of the petition, USCIS will forward the petition to the US Embassy, Manila for further processing leading to the issuance of the visa applied for.
